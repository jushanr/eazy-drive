Pass Plus Training

Pass Plus is a practical training course that takes at least six hours and helps drivers improve their skills, confidence and safety after passing their driving test.

It can be taken at any time, although it is particularly useful for new drivers during their first year of independent driving.

Watch the videos below to learn more about road awareness, safe driving and why additional training can help you stay focused behind the wheel.
