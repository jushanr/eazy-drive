Additional Training for Licence Holders

Roads, vehicles and driving conditions continue to change. New road layouts, updated rules, modern vehicle technology and increasing traffic can make driving feel very different from when you originally learned.

Stay Focused training is designed for full licence holders who want to refresh their knowledge, rebuild their confidence or improve their road awareness. It can also help drivers become more comfortable after a long break from driving or when moving to a new or unfamiliar vehicle.

Training is tailored to your individual needs, whether you have only recently passed or have been driving for many years. The aim is to help you adapt to today’s roads and remain safe, calm and confident behind the wheel.
